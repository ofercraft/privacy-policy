Privacy Policy for Feldman Compass

Effective Date: 18/12/2025
Developer: Feldman

Introduction

Feldman (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the Feldman Compass mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Feldman Compass is designed to function without collecting or storing personal data beyond what is strictly necessary for core functionality.

Information We Collect

Location Information

The App requests location access solely to determine device orientation and provide accurate compass functionality. Location data:

  • Is used only while the App is in use
  • Is processed locally on your device
  • Is not stored, transmitted, logged, or shared

The App does not track your location in the background.

No Other Data Collection

Feldman Compass does not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal identifiers (such as name, email address, or phone number)
  • Account information (the App has no accounts or login system)
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash reports linked to individuals

Advertising

The App does not display ads and does not integrate with any advertising networks or third-party ad services.

Third-Party Services

Feldman Compass does not use third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools that collect data from users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose user data to any third parties. Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no data to share.

Data Security

Because Feldman Compass does not collect or retain personal data, risks associated with data storage and breaches are minimized. All processing related to compass and location functionality occurs locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Feldman Compass does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or users of any age. The App is safe to use for all audiences.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App’s functionality or data practices change. Any updates will be reflected by a revised effective date and made available with the App.
