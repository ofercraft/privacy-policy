Privacy Policy – Cart Keeper
Developer: Tal Feldman
Last updated: July 17, 2025

Cart Keeper is a smart list and pantry app that allows users to manage shopping lists, pantry items, and to-dos across devices. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Account and Authentication

To use Cart Keeper, you must register with an email and password. This information is stored securely on our servers and used only for authentication and syncing your personal data.

Data We Collect

  • Email & Password: Required to create and access your account. Passwords are securely hashed before storage.
  • Your shopping lists, to-dos, and pantry items are stored on our servers to sync across your devices.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical data (e.g., device type, request logs) used to ensure app performance and improve service reliability.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To authenticate and manage your account securely.
  • To store and sync your lists across devices.
  • To improve app features and performance.
  • To send you optional updates or feature announcements.

Data Security

All data is encrypted during transfer using HTTPS. Passwords are hashed with modern cryptographic methods. We apply standard security practices to protect our systems and infrastructure.

Data Sharing

Your data is not sold or shared with advertisers. It may be shared only with trusted service providers (e.g., for cloud hosting) under strict agreements and only as necessary to provide the service.

Your Rights

  • View or edit your data from within the app.
  • Delete any list, or your entire account, at any time.
  • Request account deletion by emailing us directly.

Children’s Privacy

Cart Keeper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

Policy Updates

This policy may change over time. Major changes will be communicated in-app or via email. The latest version is always available on this page.
