Information Collection

Feldman Alert is designed to deliver real-time emergency notifications with minimum latency. Your privacy is our priority, and we do not track or save your movements. To achieve this, we collect:

  • Push Notification Tokens: FCM (Firebase) and Pushy tokens are used strictly to route alerts to your device and are stored securely on our backend.
  • Location Data: The app uses your location locally to determine if an alert is relevant to you. This data is processed strictly on your device and is never saved on or uploaded to our servers.

How Information is Used

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Delivering low-latency emergency notifications.
  • Grouping alerts based on your current or "Home" location to reduce notification fatigue (Smart Merging).
  • Improving the delivery success rate across multiple push channels (FCM & Pushy).

Third-Party Services

This application utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (FCM): To deliver push notifications using Google Infrastructure.
  • Pushy: To provide a high-priority MQTT fallback for notification delivery.
  • MapLibre: For map visualization and polygon rendering of alert areas.

Data Security & Retention

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Registration tokens are transient and can be refreshed or revoked at any time by clearing application data or resetting your device profile. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information across our backend infrastructure.
